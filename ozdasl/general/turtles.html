OzDASL

Plasma Protein of Fasting Turtles

Keywords: repeated measures

Description

Four male and four female turtles had their plasma protein measured while they were well fed and after ten and twenty days of fasting.
Variable Description
Subject 1-8
Sex Male or Female
Fed Plasma protein while well fed (mg/ml)
Fasted10 Plasma protein after fasting 10 days
Fasted20 Plasma protein after fasting 20 days

Download

Data File (tab-delimited text)

Source

Zar, J. H. (1999). Biostatistical Analysis, Fourth Edition. Prentice-Hall International, Upper Saddle River, New Jersey. Exercise 14.4.

Analysis

This is very well behaved data. There is a change from the second to third trial, but no significant difference between the sexes.
