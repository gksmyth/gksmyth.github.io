OzDASL

Timber Data

Keywords: Multiple regression

Description

These data are for specimens of 50 varieties of timber, for modulus of rigidity, modulus of elasticity and air dried density, arranged in increasing order of magnitude of the density.

Hand D.J., Daly F., Lunn A.D., McConway K.J., Ostrowski E. (1994). A Handbook of Small Data Sets. London: Chapman & Hall. Data set 246.
