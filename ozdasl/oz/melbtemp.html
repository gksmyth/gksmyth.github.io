OzDASL

Melbourne Temperatures

Keywords: time series

Description

Daily minimum and maximum temperatures for Melbourne, from 1 January 1981 to 31 December 1990. The two February 29 leap days are excluded, so there are 10 x 365 = 3650 observations.
