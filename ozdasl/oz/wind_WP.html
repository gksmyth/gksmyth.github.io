Wind and Weather at Wilson's Promotory

Keywords: time series with covariates

Description

Weather and wind data at 3 hour intervals for Wilson's Promontory, Victoria (the most southerly point of mainland Australia) during 1989. Observations were made 7 times a day, 3 hours apart. There was a 6 hour interval from the last observation at 8 pm each day to the first observation at 2 am the following day.
